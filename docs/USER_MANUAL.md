## Getting Started

### What is the Disaster Management System?

The Disaster Management System (DMS) is a web-based platform designed to help Sri Lankan citizens:
- Report emergencies and disasters in real-time
- View active disasters and affected areas
- Make donations to support relief efforts
- Receive notifications about disasters in their area
- Track the status of their emergency reports

### System Requirements

- **Device:** Computer, tablet, or smartphone
- **Internet:** Active internet connection
- **Browser:** Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- **Location Services:** Enable GPS for accurate emergency reporting

### Accessing the System

1. Open your web browser
2. Visit: `https://dms.yourdomain.com` (replace with actual URL)
3. You will see the homepage with options to:
   - View active disasters
   - Report an emergency
   - Make a donation
   - Login/Register

## Creating an Account

### Step-by-Step Registration

1. **Navigate to Registration Page**
   - Click "Register" button in the top-right corner
   - Or go to: `https://dms.yourdomain.com/auth/register`

2. **Fill in Your Details**
   - **Full Name:** Your legal name
   - **Email Address:** A valid email you can access
   - **Password:** Minimum 6 characters (use a strong password)
   - **Phone Number:** Sri Lankan mobile number (e.g., +94771234567)
   - **Role:** Select "User" (unless you're a volunteer)

3. **Submit Registration**
   - Click "Sign Up" button
   - Wait for confirmation message

4. **Verify Your Account**
   - Check your email for verification link (if applicable)
   - Click the verification link

5. **Login**
   - You can now login with your email and password

### Registration Tips

✅ **DO:**
- Use a strong, unique password
- Provide accurate contact information
- Keep your login credentials safe

❌ **DON'T:**
- Share your password with anyone
- Use simple passwords like "123456"
- Register with fake information

## Logging In

### Login Process

1. **Go to Login Page**
   - Click "Login" button
   - Or visit: `https://dms.yourdomain.com/auth/login`

2. **Enter Credentials**
   - Email address
   - Password

3. **Click "Sign In"**
   - You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Forgot Password?

1. Click "Forgot Password?" link on login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check email for password reset link
4. Click link and create new password
5. Login with new password

### Troubleshooting Login Issues

**"Invalid credentials" error:**
- Double-check your email and password
- Ensure Caps Lock is off
- Try password reset if needed

**"Account not found" error:**
- Verify you're using the correct email
- Register if you haven't created an account

**Cannot access email:**
- Contact system administrator
- Provide proof of identity to recover account

## Reporting an Emergency

### When to Report

Report an emergency when you:
- Witness or experience a disaster (flood, fire, landslide, etc.)
- See people in immediate danger
- Notice infrastructure damage that could harm others
- Require urgent assistance

### How to Report an Emergency

#### Method 1: Quick Report (Homepage)

1. **From Homepage**
   - Click "Report Emergency" button
   - Login if prompted

2. **Fill Emergency Details**
   - **Title:** Brief description (e.g., "Flooding on Galle Road")
   - **Description:** Detailed information about the situation
   - **Location:** Address or landmark
   - **Severity:** Choose appropriate level:
     - 🟢 **LOW** - Minor issue, no immediate danger
     - 🟡 **MEDIUM** - Concerning situation
     - 🟠 **HIGH** - Serious, requires urgent attention
     - 🔴 **CRITICAL** - Life-threatening emergency

3. **Add GPS Location**
   - Click "Get Current Location" button
   - Allow location access when prompted
   - Or manually enter coordinates if known

4. **Submit Report**
   - Review all information
   - Click "Submit Report" button
   - Note your report ID for tracking

#### Method 2: From Dashboard

1. Login to your account
2. Go to Dashboard
3. Click "Create Report" button
4. Follow steps above

### After Submitting

**What Happens Next:**

1. **Immediate Confirmation**
   - You'll receive a confirmation message
   - Report ID assigned for tracking

2. **Admin Review (Within 1 hour)**
   - Emergency response team reviews your report
   - May contact you for additional information
   - Report status changes to "VERIFIED" when confirmed

3. **Response Actions**
   - Rescue operations may be coordinated
   - Relevant authorities notified
   - Other users can see the emergency on the map

4. **Resolution**
   - When situation is resolved, report status becomes "RESOLVED"
   - You'll receive a notification

### Tracking Your Reports

1. Login to your account
2. Go to "My Reports" section
3. View all your submitted reports with status:
   - 🟡 **PENDING** - Under review
   - ✅ **VERIFIED** - Confirmed by admin
   - ✔️ **RESOLVED** - Situation handled

### Emergency Report Tips

✅ **DO:**
- Be specific and accurate
- Include as much detail as possible
- Enable GPS for precise location
- Report immediately when you witness a disaster
- Update report if situation changes

❌ **DON'T:**
- Submit false or joke reports (legal consequences)
- Exaggerate the severity
- Report old/resolved incidents
- Include personal opinions or speculation

### Emergency Examples

**Good Report:**
```
Title: Flooding on Galle Road near Liberty Plaza
Description: Heavy flooding approximately 3 feet deep. 
Multiple vehicles stranded. Water rising rapidly. 
About 50 people seeking higher ground.
Location: Galle Road, Colombo 03
Severity: HIGH
```

**Poor Report:**
```
Title: Flood
Description: There's water
Location: Colombo
Severity: CRITICAL
```

## Making a Donation

### How Donations Help

Your donations support:
- Emergency relief supplies (food, water, medicine)
- Temporary shelters for displaced families
- Medical care for injured victims
- Rebuilding damaged infrastructure
- Long-term recovery programs

### Donation Process

1. **Navigate to Donations Page**
   - Click "Donate" in navigation menu
   - Or visit: `https://dms.yourdomain.com/donate`

2. **Select Disaster (Optional)**
   - Choose specific disaster to support
   - Or select "General Relief Fund" for all disasters

3. **Enter Donation Amount**
   - Minimum: LKR 100
   - Suggested amounts: LKR 500, 1,000, 5,000, 10,000
   - Or enter custom amount

4. **Provide Donor Information**
   - Full name (or "Anonymous")
   - Email address (for receipt)
   - Phone number
   - Optional message of support

5. **Complete Payment**
   - Click "Proceed to Payment"
   - Choose payment method:
     - Credit/Debit Card
     - Bank Transfer
     - Mobile Payment
   - Follow payment provider instructions

6. **Confirmation**
   - Receive email confirmation
   - Get tax receipt (if applicable)
   - Track donation status in your account

### Donation Tracking

**For Registered Users:**
1. Login to your account
2. Go to "My Donations"
3. View all donations with:
   - Amount donated
   - Disaster supported
   - Date
   - Payment status
   - Receipt download

### Tax Benefits

Donations may be tax-deductible under Sri Lankan tax law. Consult your tax advisor.

### Donation Safety

✅ **Verified System:**
- All donations tracked in secure database
- Official receipts provided
- Transparent fund allocation

🔒 **Secure Payments:**
- SSL encryption
- PCI-compliant payment processing
- No card details stored on our servers

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is the system free to use?**  
A: Yes, creating an account and reporting emergencies is completely free.

**Q: Who can see my emergency reports?**  
A: Verified reports are visible to all users and authorities. Your contact information is only shared with emergency responders.

**Q: How quickly are reports reviewed?**  
A: Most reports are reviewed within 1 hour. Critical emergencies are prioritized.

**Q: Can I report anonymously?**  
A: You must have an account to report, but your identity is protected. Only authorities see your contact details.

### Emergency Reporting

**Q: What if I report a false emergency by mistake?**  
A: Contact support immediately. Intentional false reports may result in account suspension.

**Q: Can I upload photos with my report?**  
A: Currently, photos are not supported. Provide detailed descriptions instead. (Feature planned for future)

**Q: What if my location is wrong?**  
A: You can edit the location manually or enter coordinates directly.

**Q: How do I update my report?**  
A: Login, go to "My Reports", click the report, and select "Edit" (if still pending).

### Donations

**Q: Is my donation tax-deductible?**  
A: Consult a tax professional. We provide official receipts that may qualify.

**Q: How is my donation used?**  
A: Funds go directly to disaster relief - emergency supplies, shelters, medical care, and rebuilding.

**Q: Can I donate items instead of money?**  
A: Currently only monetary donations accepted online. Contact local relief centers for item donations.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**  
A: Yes. We use encrypted payment processing. No card details are stored on our servers.

### Account Issues

**Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?**  
A: Use "Forgot Password" link on login page to reset via email.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**  
A: Yes. Contact support to request account deletion.

**Q: Can I change my registered email?**  
A: Yes, in Profile Settings. You'll need to verify the new email.

## Glossary

**Disaster:** A sudden event causing significant damage, destruction, or loss of life.

**Emergency Report:** A user-submitted report of a disaster or dangerous situation.

**Severity Levels:**
- **LOW:** Minor incident, no immediate danger
- **MEDIUM:** Concerning situation requiring attention
- **HIGH:** Serious emergency needing urgent response
- **CRITICAL:** Life-threatening situation

**Verified Report:** Emergency report confirmed by administrator as authentic.

**Active Disaster:** Ongoing disaster event affecting an area.

**Rescue Operation:** Coordinated response to save lives and provide aid.

## Appendix: Disaster Preparedness Tips

### Before a Disaster

- Keep emergency contact numbers handy
- Prepare emergency kit (water, food, first aid, flashlight)
- Know evacuation routes in your area
- Stay informed about weather warnings

### During a Disaster

- Follow official evacuation orders
- Report emergencies immediately using DMS
- Stay away from damaged buildings
- Help others if it's safe to do so

### After a Disaster

- Check on family and neighbors
- Report damage using DMS
- Avoid disaster areas unless authorized
- Follow recovery instructions from authorities

### Emergency Contacts (Sri Lanka)

- **Emergency Services:** 119
- **Police:** 119
- **Fire & Rescue:** 110
- **Ambulance:** 110
- **Disaster Management Centre:** 117
